# Mentor Guide

## Overview
As a Mentor, your primary responsibility is evaluating teams based on the official grading criteria.

## Evaluations
- Use the **Evaluation Dashboard** (`/workspace/mentor/evaluation`) to view your assigned teams.
- Scores are out of 10 for each criterion (Innovation, Complexity, Impact, Presentation).
- You may pause an evaluation and resume it later.
- Once submitted, your scores automatically feed into the **Ranking Engine**.
